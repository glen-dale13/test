Constitution of United Black Students at the University of Miami Updated: November 20, 2019 Date: November 20, 2019

Articles

Article I. Name

This organization shall be named United Black Students at the University of Miami. United Black Students may also be referred to as U.B.S.

Article II. Statement of Purpose

Section 1. Guiding Principles

The purpose of United Black Students is to unite the black community here at the University of Miami, enrich the entire campus, and provide a source through which the overall University community may experience black culture and become more educated about black heritage.

Section 2. Objectives

The objectives of United Black Students shall be to:

Enhance the learning environment for black students at the University of Miami wherein these students may progress educationally by providing academic and career related information, promoting unity and awareness and a sense of responsibility, social interaction, cultural expression, and orientation.

Create a channel through which black students at the University of Miami may interact with the local community.

Article III. Affiliation Statement

This Organization shall be affiliated with the Office of Multicultural Student Affairs.

Article IV. Responsibility

United Black Student shall comply with all University of Miami policies and procedures, including but not limited to those policies set forth in the Student Rights and Responsibilities Handbook, The Student Organization Handbook, as well as local, state, and federal laws.

Article V. Membership

Section 1. General Membership

A. Membership of United Black Students shall be open to all University of Miami students in good Academic Standing, who have paid their Student Activity Fee, and are currently enrolled as a full-time student (minimum of 12 credits (undergraduate)/9 credits (graduate)). Section 2. Active Membership

A. Active Membership in United Black Students is defined as those students who have paid their yearly dues of $15 or who have paid their semester dues of $10.

B. Benefits of Active Membership include the right to vote and run for Executive Board positions (if eligibility requirements are met, see below), an organizational t-shirt, discounts to various events, and priority to any food and prize giveaways United Black Students may have.
